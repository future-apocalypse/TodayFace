# TodayFace

A minimalistic iOS application for tracking your daily mood throughout the year.

TodayFace allows you to record how you feel every day using a simple and expressive face-based interface. Over time, the app builds a visual map of your emotions across the entire year, helping you understand patterns in your life and reflect on your emotional journey.

The goal of this project is to combine **minimal design**, **smooth interactions**, and **clear emotional visualization** in a way that feels natural and enjoyable to use every day.

> ⚠️ This project is currently in development and planned for future release on the App Store.

---

# Concept

The main idea behind TodayFace is simple:

- Each day of the year is represented by a small face.
- You select the emotion that represents your day.
- Over time, the grid fills with your emotional history.

Instead of writing long journal entries, you capture the **essence of the day in one tap**.

---

# Features

Current and planned features include:

- 📅 **Yearly mood grid** showing every day of the year
- 😊 **7 emotional states** to represent your daily mood
- 📊 **Statistics view** to analyze emotional trends
- 🎨 **Theme customization**
- 📱 **Custom app icons**
- 🔔 **Optional notifications**
- 📳 **Haptic feedback support**
- ⚡ **Minimalistic and fast UI built with SwiftUI**
